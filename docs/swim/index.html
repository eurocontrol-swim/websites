What is SWIM?

SWIM is the answer to achieving global interoperability and service orientation in air traffic management.

Learn more

SWIM is also about services

Information exchanges are based on interoperable information services. SWIM supports service providers and consumers with harmonised service descriptions.

Learn more

SWIM is also about information

The use of the AIRM, the reference vocabulary for defining air traffic management information in SWIM, facilitates semantic interoperability.

Learn more

SWIM is also about technology

SWIM facilitates technical interoperability by specifying technical interfaces and the capabilities required to allow a reliable, secure and efficient exchange of information.

Learn more
