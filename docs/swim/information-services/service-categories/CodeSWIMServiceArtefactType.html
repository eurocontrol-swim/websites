CodeSWIMServiceArtefactType

http://reference.swim.aero/information-services/service-categories/CodeSWIMServiceArtefactType

A categorisation of service artefacts.

Value Definition
SERVICE_DEFINITION
 A document, issued by a community of interest, used to harmonize service implementations.
SERVICE_DESCRIPTION
 Information needed in order to use, or consider using, a service.
SERVICE_OVERVIEW
 A set of information service metadata intended to promote service discovery and an initial evaluation of the information service characteristics.
